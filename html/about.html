Welcome to Houseboard!

Houseboard is an online creative interior design magzine that combines the ideas of likebook and design board. It's created by a student team including Lilian Qian, Winni Liu and Vio Zhu.

Houseboard includes handpicked interior design posts that our audience can get inspirations from. We also have a design board for people to design their furnishing by location so that people can manage their collections better overall.

We hope you enjoy it. Please contact us if you have any feedback.
